About 

Our vision

We are Connet Information Technology, a small but passionate team driven by the vision of crafting the world best tools for aerospace engineers. CSIDE stands as our inaugural integrated development environment, setting itself apart from traditional IDEs with its lightweight nature. CSIDE is written entirely in Python 3 and provides an extensible plug-in system. Users can leverage the extensive ecosystem provided by Python to customize CSIDE to their specific needs and workflows. Inspired deeply by Eclipse and Visual Studio Code, CSIDE pays homage to these masters through its design philosophy.

We believe there's a better way to work as an engineer, which comes from a Chinese saying:
If a worker wants to do his job well, he must first sharpen his tools. So we build CSIDE starts with the following assumptions.

ASM1. A good engineer always has strong desire to sharpen his tools based on specific needs and workflow.

ASM2. A good tool even more productive than 100 assistants.
